The Story

It started with
one bodega

GoldStar was inspired by a Staten Island bodega manager who started rewarding students for good grades.

What began with one kid and one report card grew into a viral movement that brought families from across the country.

"If you can help your community the same way I've helped my community, why not?"

— The inspiration behind GoldStar

We're building on that vision — creating a nationwide platform where communities come together to recognize and reward student achievement.

Because hard work should always pay off.
